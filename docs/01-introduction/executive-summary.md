# Executive Summary

## Project Overview

MERL-T (Multi-Expert Legal Retrieval Transformer) is an innovative AI-powered architecture designed to revolutionize legal research, compliance monitoring, and regulatory analysis. The project is sponsored and governed by ALIS (Artificial Legal Intelligence Society), a non-profit association dedicated to advancing Artifcial Legal Intelligence. The system leverages cutting-edge natural language processing, knowledge graphs, and machine learning.

## Key Objectives

- **Automated Legal Research**: Streamline legal document analysis and case law research.
- **Compliance Monitoring**: Real-time regulatory compliance tracking and risk assessment.
- **Knowledge Graph Integration**: Build comprehensive legal knowledge networks.
- **AI-Act Compliance**: Ensure adherence to European AI regulations.
- **Open Source Framework**: Promote transparency, community collaboration and democratic access to legal knowledge.

## Target Impact

- Reduce legal research time.
- Improve compliance accuracy.
- Democratize legal knowledge.
- Foster legal AI innovation in compliance with european principles.

## Technology Stack

- **Backend**: FastAPI
- **Databases**:
    - **Vector Store**: ChromaDB
    - **Graph Database**: Neo4j
- **Frontend**: React/Next.js, TypeScript
- **AI/ML**:
    - **LLM Orchestration**: LangChain
    - **LLM Provider**: OpenRouter
    - **NLP**: spaCy, PyTorch
- **Infrastructure**: Docker for deployment.

## Success Metrics

- Platform adoption by legal professionals.
- Community contribution growth.
- Regulatory compliance validation.
- Research publication impact.
