# Research Papers & Documents

This directory contains research papers, preliminary analyses, and reference documents for the MERL-T project.

## Contents

### 001_Analisi Preliminare MERL-T_LAIBIT (Reg. UE 2024_1689)_RV_GP_17-04-25.pdf

**Title**: Preliminary Analysis - MERL-T and EU AI Act Regulation 2024/1689
**Authors**: RV, GP (ALIS Team)
**Date**: April 17, 2025
**Language**: Italian

**Description**: Comprehensive preliminary analysis of the MERL-T system in the context of the EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689). This document examines compliance requirements, risk assessment, and alignment strategies for the MERL-T architecture as a high-risk AI system in the legal domain.

**Key Topics**:
- EU AI Act compliance framework
- Risk classification for legal AI systems
- Technical requirements for high-risk AI
- Transparency and explainability obligations
- Governance and quality management systems
- Documentation and record-keeping requirements

### MERLT-BIBLIOGRAFIA.pdf

**Title**: MERL-T Bibliography
**Date**: 2025
**Language**: Italian

**Description**: Comprehensive bibliography of academic papers, legal frameworks, technical specifications, and reference materials that informed the design and development of MERL-T.

**Categories**:
- Legal AI and NLP research
- Reinforcement Learning from Feedback (RLHF/RLCF)
- Knowledge graph methodologies
- Legal reasoning and interpretative methods
- EU AI Act and regulatory frameworks
- Italian legal system references
